Monsoon Course On HCI 2023

IDC School of Design, IIT Bombay announces a 15-day course on human-computer interaction design from June 1-15, 2022. The course is meant for working professionals and serves as an exposure to user studies, interaction design, design evaluation and related topics. This will be a synchronous, full time course delivered in hybrid mode.

Who should attend?

This course is for you if you are:

  • A user interface designer with little formal exposure to design
  • A design professional with experience in another design field and now wants to move to interaction design
  • A software professional with experience in user interface design, but no formal training
  • A quality professional in the IT industry, who would like to learn about usability
  • A product manager responsible for the delivery of software products
  • An account executive responsible for client interaction in the IT industry
  • A faculty member in a design school or an engineering college who would like to teach a course on topics such as human-computer interaction, user studies, interaction design or design evaluation.

The course has two main goals. Firstly, it gives participants an exposure to issues, theories, principles and concerns relating to human interaction with interactive products. Secondly, participants get to practice the process and the techniques related to user studies, interaction design and design evaluation. After attending the course, participants should be able to conceptualise, design and evaluate interactive products. Those who are already involved in these activities should be able to improve the quality of their work, and should understand the theory behind what they do. Those responsible for the software development process should be able to make the process in their organizations more user-centered.

Registration

Before you register, please have handy a scanned copy of your photo-id (e.g. Adhaar card). To register, please follow the following steps:

  • Please visit https://portal.iitb.ac.in/ceqipapp/home.jsp and click on the link "List CEP/GIAN Courses".
  • Select CEP/GAIN courses. Search for the "HCI" and click on the "Apply" button below the "Monsoon Course on HCI 2022" course name. Create an account and login. Then fill out the course registration form. You will be asked to submit a photo id. Also, please make sure that you choose the appropriate category (academia, government organisation, industry, foriegn national or student).
  • Your application will be approved (usually within 24 hours), after which you will get an email
  • Login on https://portal.iitb.ac.in/ceqipapp/home.jsp again, click on "My Courses" and click on the button called "Application Submission Details".
  • Pay the fees. You could pay by internet banking, credit or debit cards, demand draft (DD), or choose to pay through your company. Internet banking / cards are the fastest (minutes), company payments are the slowest (days). If you choose DD, you will need to have the DD details ready. If you choose to use company payment, please have the name and contact details of the person in the your company who will manage the payment.
  • After we receive your fees, we will send you a confirmation of your registration.

The above process is demonstrated in this Youtube video (that version is a bit older, but still gives an overview). In case you face any problems, please write to anirudha{at the rate}iitb.ac.in.

Certificate of completion (new)
In previous editions of the Monsoon Course on HCI, the Continuing Education Programme (CEP) office of IIT Bombay gave the participants who attended the course a certificate of participation. In 2022, the CEP office will give a certificate of completion to those participants who get 50% marks or more in the course evaluations. Please refer to the FAQs for more details on certification.

Cancellation Policy
For cancellations, please write to anirudha{at the rate}iitb.ac.in. If you cancel till April 30, 2022, IIT Bombay will refund 80% of the fees paid (minus GST). If you cancel by May 15, 2022, IIT Bombay will refund 60% of the fees paid (minus GST). There will be no refunds thereafter. However, participant exchange will be allowed till May 28, 2022. In case you opt for in-person participation, and you cannot travel at the last minute (for example, due to Covid19 restrictions in your city), you can switch to virtual participation. In case Covid19 restrictions are imposed in Mumbai / IIT Bombay campus, the course will run in a fully virtual mode for all participants. In either case, refunds due to cancellations are not possible after May 15, 2022.

Accommodation
Shared accomodation is available in an IIT Bombay guest house for a limited number of participants on a first-come-first-served basis (see current tarrifs and policy - CEP course participants are Category III). If you require guest house accommodation, please indicate so when you register. Your accommodation will be confirmed by email after you pay the registration fees. (Please note that this is a manual process and there could be small delays.) Accommodation costs are not included in the registration fee. Please do not pay the accommodation cost at the time of registration - you need to pay these at the venue.
